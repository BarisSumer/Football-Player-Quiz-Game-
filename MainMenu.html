2022 Football Players Quiz Game

Purpose and Rules of The Game

You have 10 chances to guess the football player.

After each guess, feedback will be revealed, showing how close your guess is to the mystery footballer in a number of categories.

Example Guess: BRUNO FERNANDES

Means that the mystery player is not Portuguese, does not play for Manchester United. However, the player is a midfielder and 28 years old and shirt number is 8.

The purpose of the game is to find the mysterious football player using 10 guesses.

Good Luck !!!
